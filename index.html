Hello! I am a third-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at Georgetown University located in Washington, DC. I am also a member of The Massive Data Institute (MDI) working with Prof. Lisa Singh.

After working for a short time and realizing my interest in machine learning and its applications, I started pursuing Ph.D. at Georgetown University where I obtained MS degree in computer science as a path toward Ph.D.

My research mainly focus on Machine Learning, Data Science, Text Mining and Social Media Mining.
